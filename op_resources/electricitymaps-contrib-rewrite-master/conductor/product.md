# Product Definition: Electricity Maps

## Product Overview
Electricity Maps is a real-time visualization platform that provides transparency into the greenhouse gas footprint of electricity consumption worldwide. Following the 'Precision Architect' design system, it delivers an industrial, high-density interface for expert grid analysis and carbon-aware decision making.

## Target Audience
- **Environmentally Conscious Citizens:** Individuals wanting to shift their energy usage to cleaner times.
- **Policy Makers & Researchers:** Experts analyzing grid performance and decarbonization trends.
- **Software Developers:** Teams building carbon-aware applications and services.
- **Energy Providers:** Utilities seeking to communicate their generation mix and environmental impact.

## Key Features
- **Real-time Visualization:** An interactive map showing live carbon intensity and power generation mix across the globe.
- **Consumption vs. Production Accounting:** Advanced flow-tracing algorithms to attribute emissions correctly based on electricity imports and exports.
- **Historical Insights:** Access to past data for trend analysis and historical benchmarking.
- **Open Data Ingestion:** A robust system of community-driven parsers fetching data from hundreds of regional sources.
- **Multi-platform Access:** Optimized experiences across Web and Mobile (Android/iOS) via Capacitor.
- **Scalable Configuration:** Flexible YAML-based configuration for geographic zones and inter-regional exchanges.

## Value Proposition
By transforming complex grid data into intuitive, real-time insights, Electricity Maps empowers individuals and organizations to act on climate change through smarter electricity usage and informed policy decisions.
